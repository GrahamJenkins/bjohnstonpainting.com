About Bill Johnston Painting

Bill Johnston Painting is a family owned business headquarted in Lincoln Nebrsaka. For nearly two decades Bill Johnston has been painting professionally. Starting from Bill's passion for quality restoration, Bill Johnston Painting has grown to handle everything from interior and exterior painting to cleaning and restoration.

We use all quality products such as Benjamin Moore paint, California paints, Azek composite trim boards and decking, Hardi plank siding, Tyvek house wrap, Zip Wall sheathing, and many more quality products.
