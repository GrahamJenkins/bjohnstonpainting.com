About Bill Johnston Painting

Bill Johnston Painting is a family owned business headquartered in Lincoln, Nebraska. For nearly two decades Bill Johnston has been painting professionally. Starting with Bill's passion for quality restoration, Bill Johnston Painting has grown to handle everything from interior and exterior painting to cleaning and restoration.

We only use quality products such as Azek composite trim boards and decking, Zip Wall sheathing, California paints, Benjamin Moore paint, and many more.

Check out our services

At Bill Johnston, we strive for complete customer satisfaction and pride ourselves on building our business primarily from referrals and repeat customers in the greater Eastern Nebraska region.
